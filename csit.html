Year of Establishment: 1992


Mandatory Disclosure for compliance of AICTE norm- 2021-22

Welcome to the "S.o.S. in Computer Science & IT". It was established in the year 1992 under the aegis of this
University and with the support of UGC and State Government. It is working as an academic department of the
University. Currently, the Department is running two years full time Master of Computer Applications (M.C.A.) and
two years full time Master of Science in Information Technology (M.Sc.IT). Apart from this the department also
conducts Ph.D. program. The Department is also providing research facilities and presently research scholars are
working in the area of Parallel Computing and Computer Networking. Students of this department are employed
abroad as well as in reputed organisations like TCS, NIC, CHIPS, Cognizant, Infosys, L&T etc.
